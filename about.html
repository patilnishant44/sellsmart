About Us

Sellsmart is a simple and farmer-friendly platform created to provide real-time wholesale prices of crops from different market areas. Our mission is to help farmers make smart and profitable decisions by giving them quick access to the latest market rates — all in one place. With Sellsmart, there’s no need to visit multiple sources or guess prices. Just open the website, check your crop, and see where it's selling at the best rate near you. This is just the beginning — future updates will bring new features like price history, alerts, and regional language support to make Sellsmart even more helpful for farmers across India.
